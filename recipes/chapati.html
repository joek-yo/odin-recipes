Chapati

Description

Chapati is a soft, layered flatbread that is staple in East Africa and Indian causine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt and oil in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water gradually and knead into a dough.
  3. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide into small balls and roll each into a thin circle.
  5. Cook on a hot pan, flipping until golden brown
