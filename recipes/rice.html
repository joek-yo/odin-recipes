Rice

Description

Rice is a versatile staple food that can be enjoyed with a variety of dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice in water.
  2. Boil water in a pot.
  3. Add the rice and salt.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat until water is absorbed.
  5. Fluff with a fork and serve.
